Learn More About Colleen

Colleen M. McAuley is an award-winning client relationship specialist with experience as a digital strategist and web developer.

She is a member of the the Tampa Bay Women's Technology Society which provides classes and workshops in web and software development. Previously, she served as a Teaching Assistant at Girl Develop It.

Her interests include:

  • Privacy & Security
  • Data Visualization
  • Front-End Development
  • FinTech

Colleen participated in Code for America's "National Day of Civic Hacking." She also participated in the Synapse Innovation Summit's Hackathon, where her team built a chatbot utilizing the natural language processing service from IBM's Watson. Colleen is a member of Tech Ladies and Women in Tech International (WITI).

In 2018, the Gasparilla International Film Festival named Colleen their “Returning Volunteer of the Year.”
