Learn More About Colleen

Colleen M. McAuley is a Full-Stack Developer. Most recently, she was a Teaching Assistant for Girl Develop It, a non-profit organization, which provides learning opportunities in web and software development.

She attended Code Institute in Dublin, Ireland. While living in Dublin, Colleen was active in programming conferences, meetups, and volunteered for the Dún Laoghaire-Rathdown Local Enterprise Week’s Coding Workshop.

Her interests include:

  • Privacy & Security
  • Data Visualization
  • Front-End Development
  • FinTech

Recently, Colleen participated in Code for America's "National Day of Civic Hacking." She also participated in the Synapse Innovation Summit's Hackathon, where her team built a chatbot utilizing the artificial intelligence from IBM's Watson for the NLP. In addition to being involved with Girl Develop It, Colleen is also a member of Tech Ladies and Women in Tech International (WITI).

In 2018, the Gasparilla International Film Festival named Colleen their “Returning Volunteer of the Year.”
